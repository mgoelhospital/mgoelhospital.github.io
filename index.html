about us

M Goel Hospital was established in 1994. It is a multi-specialty hospital managed by Dr. SUSHIL GOEL, who is a General & Laparoscopic Surgeon. The hospital has been specifically promoted as the Surgical Centre of Excellence where all types of operations are performed in the hospital.

Hospital has a team of well qualified and dedicated doctors and fully trained paramedical staff. Our hospital is dedicated to provide comprehensive health care including preventive, diagnostic, therapeutic services. General administration of hospital is taken care by Mrs. Meena Goel.

The main motive of the hospital is to take care of the smallest needs of the patients with complete dedication and compassion. Our aim is to provide quality healthcare to all the patient at affordable prices.

DEPARTMENTS

L

General and Laparoscopic Surgery

It is looked after by senior consultant Dr. Sushil Goel. Hospital is fully equipped to perform all sorts of laparoscopic surgeries. Some of the common operations performed in the hospital include laparoscopic appendisectomy, laparoscopic cholecystectomy, heamoroidectomy, fissurectomy and hernia surgeries.

G

Gynaecology

We perform all kinds of gynaecological surgeries using the most advanced laparoscopic and minimally invasive techniques. We perform operations such as laparoscopic hysterectomy â€“ LAVH, TLH, non-descent vagional hysterectomy, vaginal hysterectyomy for prolapse and cystocoele, myomectomy (fibroid surgery), ovarian cyst surgery, infertility management.

E

Urology and Endoscopic Stone Removal

We perform minimally invasive operations for a variety of urological problems, which produce excellent results with short hospital stays, less discomfort & less bleeding. Some of the surgeries performed here include PCNL for kidney stones, URS for ureteric stones, endoscopic bladder stone removal, IOU for stricture urethra & TURP for prostate enlargement.

S

Skin Treatment

It is looked after by senior consultant Dr. Vikas Khera, who is working as a senior dermatologist in Gurgaon since 1995. He provides skin consultation from 11:00 am to 1:00 pm from Monday to Friday. Appointment can be taken from 9:00 am onwards for that day.

M

Minor Surgeries and Procedures

Hospital is specially equipped to perform day care minor procedures. Surgeries like sebacious cyst, dermoid cyst, small abscess, small lipoma, ingrowing nails, circumcision etc. are performed as day-care procedures at a very affordable price with short stay.

P

Pharmacy

The hospital has in-house pharmacy which caters to all the needs of the indoor and outdoor patients.

our team

We have a team of highly dedicated and well-qualified doctors

Dr. Sushil Goel, General and Laproscopic Surgeon

He finished his graduation and post-graduation from MDU, Rohtak. He has the specialization and experience of 30 years in doing major and minor surgeries of all kinds. Highly devoted, hardworking and dedicated doctor, he is always available for his patients and provides them with utmost care.

Dr. Vikas Khera, Skin Specialist

He completed his post-graduation in dermatology, venereology and leprology from AIIMS in 1992. Afterwards, he worked as a research associate in AIIMS where his pioneering study on "Vitiligo" was published in international journal of dermatology. He is in private practice as a consultant dermatologist since 1995.

Dr. Kapoor, Anaesthetist

He did his MBBS from the Medical College, Allahabad. Afterwards, he joined Medical College, Amritsar as a research officer on the project "Anaemia and anaesthesia, biochemical and cardiovascular study" and also completed his MD. Worked as a specialist in Moolchand hospital till 1982 and then started private anaesthesia practice in South Delhi and now continuing his practice in Gurgaon.
